The Bro Split

Focuses on one muscle group per day, allowing for deep isolation and maximizing growth. Ideal for bodybuilders aiming for detailed muscle development.

Day 1: Chest

Barbell Bench Press - 4 sets of 8-10 reps
Incline Dumbbell Press - 4 sets of 10-12 reps
Chest Dips - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Cable Flyes - 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Day 2: Back

Deadlifts - 4 sets of 5 reps
Pull-ups - 4 sets of 8-10 reps
Bent-over Rows - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Lat Pulldowns - 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Day 3: Shoulders

Overhead Barbell Press - 4 sets of 8-10 reps
Lateral Raises - 4 sets of 10-12 reps
Front Raises - 3 sets of 12 reps
Reverse Pec Deck Flyes - 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Day 4: Arms

Barbell Curls - 4 sets of 8-10 reps
Dumbbell Hammer Curls - 3 sets of 10-12 reps
Triceps Dips - 4 sets of 10-12 reps
Skull Crushers - 3 sets of 10 reps

Day 5: Legs

Squats - 4 sets of 8-10 reps
Leg Press - 4 sets of 10-12 reps
Romanian Deadlifts - 3 sets of 8-10 reps
Leg Curls - 3 sets of 12-15 reps
